Frequently Asked Questions

To add a new product, navigate to the Products page and click the "Add Product" button. Fill in all the required information including product name, category, price, and stock quantity. You can also upload an image for better product identification.

The Dashboard provides an overview of your sales metrics, including total earnings, balance, and sales trends. For detailed income tracking, visit the Income page where you can view all transactions, monthly trends, and export reports.

Go to the Customers page to view all customer information. You can add new customers by clicking "Add Customer" and filling in their details. Use the search function to quickly find specific customers.

If you experience any technical issues, try refreshing the page first. If the problem persists, contact our support team using the contact information provided in the support section below.

Regular backups are automatically performed. You can also export your data from various sections like Income and Products. For complete data backup, contact our support team.
